# Epic 03 manual test

1. Enable audio and select a pitched voice.
2. Play `Z X C V B N M`; confirm ascending white notes C4 through B4.
3. Play `S D G H J`; confirm the corresponding lower black notes.
4. Play `Q W E R T Y U I O P`; confirm ascending upper white notes C5 through E6.
5. Play `2 3 5 6 7 9 0`; confirm the corresponding upper black notes.
6. Hold a key through operating-system repeat; confirm it produces only one voice.
7. Play multiple computer keys together; confirm polyphonic sound and matching highlights.
8. Play and release each on-screen key with a pointer. Drag or cancel away and confirm no note sticks.
9. Hold the same note from the computer and on-screen controls. Release one source; the other must continue and remain highlighted.
10. Choose a new waveform from the selector, then immediately play mapped keys; keyboard playing must resume after selection.
11. Hold a note and open the waveform selector before releasing the key; the note must stop when the selector opens.
12. Hold notes and change tab or window focus; all notes must release.
13. Start several notes and select **Stop sound**; all notes and highlights must clear.
